Dear valued user,

Thank you for choosing to shop through us and for using our cashback program. We understand that you are waiting for your cashback to be tracked. Please note that it can take 48-72 hours for cashback to be tracked.

In case your transaction was not tracked even after this duration, please raise a Missing Cashback Claim for the transaction that was not tracked. Please note that merchants accept claims for cashback not tracked cases only till the 20th day from the purchase date.

To raise a Missing Cashback Claim, please follow these steps:

  • Log in to your Earnow account
  • Go to the "Profile" section
  • Click on "Missing Cashback Claim"
  • Fill out the required information and submit your claim

Video Explanation: https://youtu.be/Dno7fLW3Vtw

Please note that Flipkart currently does not accept Missing Cashback Claims for orders placed on their platform. Therefore, we are unable to process any Missing Cashback Claims for Flipkart orders.

If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact us. We are here to assist you.
